Bob the Unicorn

Become the prettiest unicorn in unicorn land

Bob the Unicorn is a little 2-level game I developed with Unity and C# for a school project. Eager to build a game entirely from the ground up, I challenged myself with designing all the game assets alongside the development of gameplay. The game features camera and player control systems, menus, enemies, and powerups.
